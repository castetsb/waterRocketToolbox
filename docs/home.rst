The Water ROcket Toolbox is a collection of tools and resources related to the book "Carnet de fusée à eau de tonton Benoit".

#Documentation: `pyRobotiqGripper Documentation <https://pyrobotiqgripper.readthedocs.io/en/latest/>`_

.. warning::
    Water rocket involve high pressure and can be dangerous if not handled properly.
    Always follow safety guidelines and use appropriate protective equipment when building
    and flying water rockets.
